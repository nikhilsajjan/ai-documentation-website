> what does influenceRadius do? 

‚è∫ influenceRadius controls how far from your cursor/finger the dots are affected.

  const influenceRadius = isDragging ? 20 : 10;

  What it does:
  - Defines a circular "zone of influence" around the cursor/touch point
  - Dots inside this radius: get displaced, scale up, and brighten
  - Dots outside this radius: only do the ambient wave animation

  Values:
  - Normal hover: 10 units (smaller area)
  - When dragging: 20 units (2x larger area, more dots affected)

  Visual effect:
  - Smaller radius = precise, focused interaction (fewer dots move)
  - Larger radius = broader, cloth-like effect (more dots move together)

  Think of it like touching water - influenceRadius is how wide the ripples spread from your finger.
